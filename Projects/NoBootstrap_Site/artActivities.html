Saenger Theatre

One of the biggest performing arts buildings in all of Pensacola! I used to go here a lot back when I was a student in drama, it's very gorgeous and deserves all its praise. To visit the Saenger Theatre website, Click here.


Pensacola Little Theatre

Pensacola's finest aspiring talents who are always putting up great shows frequently! There are many great and affordable shows, like improvisation acts for instance, that the people love to do weekly so check them out! To visit the Pensacola Little Theatre website, Click here.


Cordova Lanes


A very nice and secluded bowling alley where fun is an absolute certainty. They stay open relatively late and the prices are pretty reasonable for what you get out of this place. To visit Cordova Lanes' website, Click here.
